# Find the Root Cause

When a problem appears, investigate until you understand why it happens. Do not suppress symptoms or assume from surface signals.

- Reproduce or observe the problem, inspect the underlying signal, and compare against the source of truth before deciding what failed.
- Ask "what would make this stop happening at all?" before reaching for a flag, filter, workaround, or explanation.
- If the root cause or fix is complex, explain the options and trade-offs before proceeding.
- Treat the problem as unresolved until the chosen fix is verified on the affected paths.
